Vote Helper for 2 College Brothers!

This page helps you easily add a bookmarklet to your browser to quickly vote for 2 College Brothers in their 3 categories.

Drag this link to your Bookmarks Bar:
Vote for 2CB (Drag Me!)

How to Use This Bookmarklet:

  1. On Desktop: Drag the "Vote for 2CB (Drag Me!)" link above directly onto your browser's Bookmarks Bar. (If you don't see the Bookmarks Bar, press Ctrl+Shift+B on Windows/ChromeOS or Cmd+Shift+B on Mac).
  2. On Mobile (More tricky):
    • Tap and hold the "Vote for 2CB (Drag Me!)" link.
    • Choose "Copy Link Address" or "Copy URL".
    • Create a new bookmark manually in your mobile browser. For the bookmark's address/URL, paste the copied code (it will start with `javascript:`).
  3. Go to the voting website: https://tampabaybestofthebest.com/vote/#
  4. Click the "Vote for 2CB (Drag Me!)" bookmarklet that you saved.
  5. The script will attempt to navigate and make selections. If it navigates to the site first (because you weren't on it), click the bookmarklet again once the voting page is loaded.
  6. After the script runs (it will show an alert: "All actions attempted..."), please review your ballot on the voting site and then click the final "Submit Voting Ballot" button yourself.

This bookmarklet automates selecting the three specified entries for 2 College Brothers. You are responsible for the final submission.
